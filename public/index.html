Who we are

Rooted in the forest, inspired by community.

Forest Trail Scouts Troop 1941 is a welcoming troop that blends classic Scouting traditions with modern skills — from knot-tying and first aid to digital navigation. Parents, leaders, and scouts work together to keep adventures safe, meaningful, and fun.

  • Monthly campouts
  • Quarterly service days
  • Merit badge workshops
